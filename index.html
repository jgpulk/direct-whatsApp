DirectWhatsapp

Send a WhatsApp message direct without adding to your contact list

Looking to send a WhatsApp message without the need to save the phone number? That's precisely the purpose of this WhatsApp direct service. If you wish to send a message without the hassle of adding the recipient to your contact list, this service offers the solution. It's designed for those who want to avoid cluttering their contacts with every person they've ever messaged on WhatsApp.
